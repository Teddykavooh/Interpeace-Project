Donors and Strategic Partners:

NEPCOH-Trust is funded by Interpeace through extensive programme supported by Foreign Federal Office of Germany (GFFO), European Union (EU), Swiss Agency for Development Cooperation(SDC) among others.

Our strategic partners

NEPCOH Trust has strategic collaborations with the County Governments and the National Government of Kenya, particularly in the FCDC counties, and substantial assistance from international donors make it feasible for the organization and its partners to undertake peacebuilding initiatives in the region.

Key partners include:

     National Cohesion and Integration Commission (NCIC)  Interpeace  County and national governments (peace departments)  County Peace Actors Forums (PAF)  Local peacebuilding structures: ceasefire monitoring committees (CMCs),  Inter-Village dialogue space committees (IVDSCs)  National Steering Committee on Peacebuilding and Conflict Management (NSC)  Frontier Counties Development Council (FCDC)  IGAD’s cross border CEWERUs Focal Points
